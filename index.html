About me

Hi there! My name is Rupesh Shirose. Currently, I am doing my Masters in the field of Electrical Engineering at University of Applied Sciences Upper Austria, Wels. At Univerisy, I learned about various power electronic converters and performed experimental analysis as well as simulation. In previous semester, I worked on project for development of soft switching circuit to reduce switching losses. Therefore, building a half bridge circuit with gate driver IC to get deadtime and LLC resonant tank to achieve zero voltage switching (ZVS) to compare it with simulation results. In addition to power electronics, I studied subjects including high voltage, measurements and sensor systems, control engineering, energy automation and protection systems.

My interests are projects involving Electrical engineering, Power electronics, Electric drives, Industrial automation, PLC/CNC machine programming, IOT and Robotics. I also have 4+ years of experence working as a Process Engineer in Projects at Asahi India Glass Ltd, which is one of the leading glass manufacturing and processing company in India. I have good understanding of the electrical hardware and electrical schematics involving switchgears, PLCs, CNCs, VFDs and servo drives. Moreover, I am familiar with PLC programming language (Ladder) and software (Siemens TIA Portal). I also know EPLAN for making electrical schematics.
